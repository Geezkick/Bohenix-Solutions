Our Premium Features

Discover how Bohenix Solutions is revolutionizing transportation and energy infrastructure in Kenya.

Collision Prevention

Our Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) communication system provides real-time alerts about potential collisions, dangerous road conditions, and emergency braking events up to 300 meters ahead.

Smart Charging Network

With 50+ solar-powered charging stations across Kenya, our network provides fast charging (0-80% in 30 minutes) with intelligent load balancing to prevent grid overload.

Fleet Analytics

Get detailed insights into driver behavior, energy consumption, maintenance needs, and route optimization with our AI-powered analytics dashboard.

Road Condition Monitoring

Our vehicle-mounted sensors detect and report potholes, flooding, and other hazards to both drivers and municipal authorities in real-time.

Carbon Footprint Tracking

Monitor your fleet's environmental impact with precise emissions tracking and receive automated sustainability reports for compliance and ESG reporting.

Enterprise Security

End-to-end encryption, blockchain-based data integrity, and multi-factor authentication ensure your fleet data remains secure from cyber threats.
